# Demo video script

Target: 4–5 minute screen recording with voiceover. Hits every judging
criterion (Tech, Presentation, Business Value, Originality).

---

### [0:00 – 0:15] Hook

**VO:** "Film investors lose billions annually on projects that should never
have been greenlit. GreenLight AI changes that."

**Visual:** Title card with logo and tagline.

---

### [0:15 – 0:45] The problem

**VO:** "A producer is considering a $7.2 million sci-fi thriller called
*The Deep Horizon*. Before investing, they need answers — is the budget
realistic? Is the market right? Is this team capable? Today, this takes
weeks of consultants and tens of thousands in fees."

**Visual:** Open the Streamlit dashboard. Show the Upload page. Click
**Load 'The Deep Horizon' demo data**.

---

### [0:45 – 1:00] Trigger analysis

**VO:** "With GreenLight AI, they click one button. Seven specialized AI
agents — built with four different framework adapters and powered by three
different LLM providers — are activated simultaneously in a Band chat room."

**Visual:** Click **🚀 Run GreenLight Analysis**. Switch to the **Debate**
page. Show the @mention fan-out message at the top of the room.

---

### [1:00 – 2:00] Phase 1 — reports roll in

**VO:** "In under two minutes, five specialists deliver their reports.
Watch them work in parallel."

**Visual:** Time-lapse / fast-forward the agent messages as they post.
Pause on each highlight:
- @ScriptAnalyst: *"45 VFX shots, EXTREME complexity 9/10, requires marine
  unit YES"*
- @BudgetAuditor: *"Marine Unit: $0 — MISSING. E&O Insurance: $0 — MISSING.
  Completion Bond: $0 — MISSING."*
- @TalentScout: *"Director has zero sci-fi experience. Star power: NONE."*

---

### [2:00 – 3:00] Phase 2 — the money shot

**VO:** "Now comes what makes GreenLight unique. The RedTeam agent reads
*all* reports — and finds what no single agent could see alone."

**Visual:** Scroll to the RedTeam challenges. Highlight C-1:

> 🔴 **CHALLENGE C-1 to @BudgetAuditor**
> VFX budget is $46K/shot but @ScriptAnalyst identified 45 shots of
> underwater CG work requiring $80–150K/shot. You're short by at least
> $1.5 million.

Then show @BudgetAuditor's revision:

> ✅ **REVISED:** Reclassifying VFX budget from ADEQUATE to CRITICALLY
> UNDERFUNDED. Adjusted Budget Adequacy Score from 6/10 to 3/10.

**VO:** "The Red Team just discovered a $1.5 million shortfall that only
emerged by comparing the script analysis against the budget. No single
agent could have caught this."

---

### [3:00 – 3:15] Dynamic recruitment (optional)

**VO:** "And when RedTeam spots a gap no existing agent covers — it recruits
a specialist on the fly using Band's agent discovery."

**Visual:** Show RedTeam's specialist recruitment message:

> 🔵 **SPECIALIST RECRUITED:** Added @SpecialistOnCall to address tax
> incentive question for California production.

---

### [3:15 – 3:45] Phase 3 — the verdict

**VO:** "Finally, the Chief Risk Officer synthesizes everything — including
all the revisions from cross-examination — into an investment-grade risk
scorecard."

**Visual:** Switch to the **Scorecard** page. Show:
- Verdict: 🟡 **CONDITIONAL**
- Score: 52/100
- Radar chart of the five component scores
- Top 5 risks
- Conditions for approval

---

### [3:45 – 4:15] The scorecard

**VO:** "Verdict: CONDITIONAL. Invest only if the producer adds $1.5
million to VFX, budgets a marine unit, adds a completion bond, and brings
in an experienced VFX supervisor. All in under five minutes — and every
finding is traceable to a specific document line."

---

### [4:15 – 4:30] Close

**Visual:** Architecture diagram.

**VO:** "GreenLight AI. Seven agents. Four frameworks. Three providers.
One Band room. Zero vendor lock-in. The AI investment committee that
never sleeps."

**End card:** GitHub link + Band logo.
